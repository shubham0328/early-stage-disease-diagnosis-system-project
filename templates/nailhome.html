Nail Diseases

Common Nail Diseases

Nails can be affected by various conditions that may indicate underlying health issues. Here are some common nail diseases:

  • Fungal Infections: Often cause discoloration, thickening, and crumbling of the nail.
  • Psoriasis: Leads to pitting, ridges, or separation of the nail from the nail bed.
  • Onycholysis: The nail lifts from the nail bed, often due to trauma or infection.

Early detection is key to managing nail diseases. Use our prediction tool to check if your nail might be affected.
