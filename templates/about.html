About Us

The Nail Disease Prediction project aims to help individuals identify potential nail diseases using advanced machine learning techniques. By uploading an image of your nail, our Hannah will analyze it for you.

Our model is built using the VGG16 deep learning architecture, trained on a dataset of nail images to accurately classify them into categories like "Healthy" or "Inflected." This tool is designed to assist users in early detection and encourage them to seek medical advice if necessary.
